---

PURPOSE

Scoping visit for a potential network infrastructure assessment. PRISD reached out after the
2018 conference. They're running infrastructure that hasn't been touched since 2013 and they
know it. Budget cycle starts in October - they want an assessment complete before then.

---

SITE OBSERVATIONS

Physical
- Three buildings on main campus: Admin (408), Cafeteria/Gym connector, STEM wing (2019 addition)
- MDF is in a converted supply closet in Admin. No dedicated power, no UPS, ambient temp
  is whatever the hallway is. Have seen worse.
- IDF in STEM wing is actually well-done - clearly built when the wing was, proper rack,
  dedicated circuit, wall-mount UPS.
- No IDF in cafeteria connector - long cable runs from Admin MDF. Worth documenting.

Network (observed, not tested)
- Core switch: unmanaged 48-port in MDF. Make unknown, no visible label. Probably 10 years old.
- Four access points visible: two in Admin, two in STEM wing. No APs in gym or cafeteria.
  Wireless dead zone in the largest assembly space.
- No visible firewall appliance. Asked Landry - they use the ISP-provided router/modem combo.
  Confirmed: no stateful inspection, no content filtering at the network layer.
- Student network and staff network are on the same physical switch. No VLAN separation observed.
  Landry confirmed they've never had segmentation.

Staff
- 2-person IT team. Landry + one part-time tech (student intern, seniors only).
- No network monitoring. They find out about outages when teachers call.

---

PRELIMINARY FINDINGS (scoping only - not final)

Priority observations:
1. No network segmentation - student and staff traffic mixed. Not unusual for this size but
   needs to be addressed.
2. No firewall. ISP CPE is not a security appliance. This is the biggest gap.
3. MDF environment inadequate - heat, power, physical security.
4. Wireless coverage gaps in cafeteria and gym.
5. No monitoring. They are flying blind.

Not observations, but worth noting:
- The STEM wing IDF is proof they can do this right when they build new. The Admin MDF
  situation is inertia, not ignorance.
